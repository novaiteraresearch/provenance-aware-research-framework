# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, rigorous, and respectful research environment,
we pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Demonstrating methodological rigor and intellectual honesty
- Providing constructive and evidence-based feedback
- Respecting differing scientific perspectives and approaches
- Accepting responsibility for errors and correcting them transparently

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory comments
- Misrepresentation of research findings or provenance
- Deliberate introduction of false or tampered artifacts
- Plagiarism or attribution fraud

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the maintainer. All reports will be reviewed
promptly and handled confidentially. Appropriate consequences will be applied
to confirmed violations.
